SKYKY CULTURAL MEDIA LTD

Skyky Cultural Media Ltd is a Canadian based company engaged in the exploration and development of education, learning and the arts. The company serves as a unique platform to bridge the communication gap of a multicultural society, and to integrate cultural resources from all over the world.

The company’s core values revolve around the themes: creativity, invention, and innovation. Our goal is to provide various professional services, such as, conferences on scholarships and studying abroad, business training, online language tutoring, organizing educational visits for foreign students, summer camps, arts classes (drawing, painting, drama etc.).......

SKYKY PUBLISHING

Skyky Children is a world of fairy tales created by Skyky Cultural Publishers Ltd. We create an art space for children to grow happily while also building a spiritual communication bridge for parents and children.

All the stories focus on the topic of love and environmental protection. With unique creation, super-imagination, and diligent observation, we explore the world from a child’s perspective.

Skyky Children stands in the intersection of the Eastern and Western world, blending different cultural concepts into our stories. The stories mostly involve animals. Through these animal characters, children are able to absorb the main idea of love from the author—love between parents and children, love for nature, and love for life.

All the tales are written for Chinese and English children with lots of humor to connect with each child. The beautiful water colour illustrations are the work of a talented child..........
